USB tethering

You can tether your phone to your computer with a USB cable to share your phone's Internet connection with your computer
  • You cannot connect your phone memory card on your computer in Mass storage or Media Transfer mode

Pocket WiFi

You can turn your phone into a portable Wi-Fi hotspot to share your phone's Internet connection with one or more computers or other devices
  • When your phone is serving as a Wi-Fi hotspot, you cannot use your phone's applications to access the Internet via its Wi-Fi connection

  • You configure the hotspot with Pocket WiFi settings
